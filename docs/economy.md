# Economy of the Game

## Cities

- Cities are the primary source of income for a faction. 
- Each city a faction controls generates 1 credit every 5 minutes.

## Credits

- Credits may be spent on units, based on that unit's cost.
- Units purchased may be deployed at any city controlled by the purchasing faction.
- Once a unit is deployed, that city cannot produce any more units until a cooldown of 5 minutes completes.
