# Economy of the Game

## Cities

- Cities are the primary source of income for a faction. 
- Each city a faction controls generates 1 credit every 5 minutes.

## Credits

- Credits may be spent on units, based on that unit's cost.
- Units purchased may be deployed at any city controlled by the purchasing faction.
- Once a unit is deployed, that city cannot produce any more units until a cooldown of 5 minutes completes.

## Purchases

- Pressing the 'P' key will open the purchase menu.
- The purchase menu will display a list of available units and their costs.
- The purchase menu will also display the current credit balance of the faction.
- The purchase menu will allow the player to select a unit to purchase.
    - The player may only purchase one unit at a time.
    - The player may only purchase a unit if they have enough credits to cover the cost.
    - The player may only purchase a unit if they have a city that is not currently producing a unit.
    - The player may only purchase a unit if they have a city that is not currently pn production cooldown.
- To purchse a unit, press the 'P' key, select the unit, and press the 'Enter' key.
- Pressing the 'Esc' key will close the purchase menu.
- Once a unit is purchased the players must then select an available city to deploy the unit.
- Available cities are shown as bright colored 3x3 tiles matching the location of the city.
- The mouse position is followed by a ghost icon image until the user clicks a city location or presses the 'Esc' key to cancel the deployment, in which case the purchase will be cancelled and no credits deducted.
- If the deployment is successful, the unit will be deployed to the selected city and the purchase will be completed, deducting the cost of the unit from the faction's credit balance.
- After a unit is deployed, that city is placed in a cooldown state for 5 minutes, during which time it cannot produce any more units.
