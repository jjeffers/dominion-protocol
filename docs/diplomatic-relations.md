# Diplomatic Relations
- Every country will have an opinion of your faction, which will be displayed in the diplomacy panel.
- Opinion is measured on a scale of +100 to -100.
- When a faction uses a nuclear weapon, all other factions will have their opinion of the launching faction reduced by 10.
- Once a country's opinion of your faction drops below 50, an allied country will become neutral. (They leave your faction.)
- Once a country's opinion of your faction drops below -50, they will become hostile. (They join an enemy faction.)
- If a country joins a faction, that country's opinion of your faction will increase to 50.
- At start all countries have a diplomatic opinion of 0.

## Invasions
- If a faction moves land units into a neutral country, that country's opinion will decay at 10 points per 10 seconds.
    - The game will issue a news messages about the violation of neutrality, naming the country violated and the violating faction.
- Capturing any of the neutral country's cities will drop that country's opinion to -100. (This will trigger that contry to join another enemy faction.)

## Neutrals joining a faction
- A country that joins a faction generates revenue for that faction, all cities become owned by that faction for purposes of production and deployment, can be used as bases for air units, and repairs units of that faction.

## Allied countries leaving a faction
- If a country leaves a faction, that country and its cities will become neutral.

## Diplomatic Status Display
- A list of all countries and their diplomatic opinion is displayed on the left hand side of the display. It is positioned between the top game status and the bottom terrain and unit status displays.
- If the list is larger than the display panel a scroll bar is provided to allow a user to view all countries.
- The list is sorted by most favorabl, then least favorable, then all other countries with an opinion of 0.
