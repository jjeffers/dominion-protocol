# Capturing Cities

- An enemy city is captured when a land unit sits within the 3x3 area of a city AND there is no enemy land or sea unit also within the 3x3 area of the city.
- Once a city is captured, it becomes the property of the faction that captured it, earning income for the owning faction.
- When a city is captured, the region associated with that city also switches ownership to the capturing faction. Borders for the faction will update to reflect the new ownership.
